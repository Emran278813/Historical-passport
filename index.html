About the Passport Program

A free, family-friendly digital scavenger hunt designed to bring Downtown Youngstown to life. Visit local attractions, complete fun challenges, and collect virtual stamps!

HOW IT WORKS

1.Choose a Destination

Pick a local spot to visit.

2.Complete Tasks

Fun games,photos & trivia await!.

3.Earn Rewards

Collect badges and unlock surprises.




Affiliated Organizations

🎨The Butler Institute of American Art.

Explore American art collections

🪨Clarence R. Smith Mineral Museum.

Explore stunning mineral and rock specimens from around the world.

🏛️Mahoning Valley Historical Society, Arms Family Museum.

Step back in time with historic exhibits inside a beautifully preserved mansion.

📚Mahoning Valley Historical Society, Tyler History Center.

Uncover Youngstown’s rich history through interactive exhibits and archives.

🖼️McDonough Museum of Art.

Experience contemporary art exhibits by regional, national, and student artists.

🧬Melnick Medical Museum.

Explore the history of medicine with fascinating artifacts and exhibits.

🔬 OH WOW! The Roger & Gloria Jones Children’s Center for Science & Technology.

Engage kids in hands-on science, tech, and fun-filled learning experiences.

🎭 SMARTS (Students Motivated by the Arts).

Inspire creativity through free art, music, dance, and theater programs for youth.

🏭 Steel Museum, Youngstown Historical Center of Industry and Labor.

Discover Youngstown’s industrial heritage and the legacy of steel manufacturing.

✨ Ward Beecher Planetarium.

Explore the cosmos with immersive star shows and astronomy exhibits.

🌳 Wean Park

Relax and enjoy scenic walking trails, playgrounds, and beautiful green spaces.

📚 Public Library of Youngstown and Mahoning County

Explore books, digital resources, and family programs at this historic and innovative library hub.

🛍️ Youngstown Flea

Shop local vendors offering handmade, vintage, and artisan goods at this popular open-air market.
